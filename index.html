How it works

1
Browse deals — Open the Sale Tracker. Items load from a curated demo catalogue covering 9 categories and 35+ products, sorted by biggest discount by default.
2
Filter & search — Use category tabs to narrow by department, type in the search box to find a specific product, or filter to your saved watchlist only.
3
Save items — Click the heart button on any card to add it to your watchlist (stored in your browser). The tracker highlights watched items and groups them together.
4
Go live — Run the included target_proxy.js Node.js server, flip LIVE = true in tracker.html, and the page will pull real-time Target sale data every 30 minutes automatically.
